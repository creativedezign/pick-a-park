Jane Metlikovec

As manager of the digital communications team, Jane is the spiritual leader of the creeps, and the original Feature Creeper. As a youngster she represented Victoria in table tennis. Now she uses up her seemingly endless reserves of energy wrangling her four-year-old daughter and thinking up new features to add to our project.

One of her favourite shows in My Kitchen Rules, ostensibly because she likes cooking, but in reality because she likes Manu. Pav spends a significant amount of time saying no to Jane’s ideas.


Cass Allan

Cass has a degree in fine arts and and is completing a masters in curating. Her interest lies in curating fine art and digital art to create a convergence of the traditional and the modern. As a fine artist, you’d think she would be a perfectionist. You’d be wrong.

Dan Morrison

Dan is a team anomaly as he is a male, and doesn’t work for DET. As a software engineer, he fills out the other slot as co head-developer. Dan is a vegan and a runner, who likes to run in Melbourne’s parks. This not only allows him to keep fit and enjoy our cities green spaces, it also gives him the opportunity to forage for dinner. 


Jeff Hoogenboom

The team’s second token male, Jeff works at DET creating video, which often consists of denying requests to film three-hour long seminars.
